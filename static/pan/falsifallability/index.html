Problem with faith, "I believe independent of the evidence", hard to imagine evidence which could change belief.

It's a struggle to ensure we choose beliefs that are falsifiable, it doesn't appear to be in our nature, in part because being wrong is hard but worthwhile.

So, what evidence could convince me I am wrong?

Almost anything, for the beliefs that are weakly held. e.g. 'Junk' DNA.

What would it take to convince me that my old beliefs are accurate?

Quite a bit. I'd need a theory that explained the kinds of evidence the world is full of better than the alternative theory.
